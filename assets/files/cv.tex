\begin{rSection}{Summary}
I am a Ph.D. researcher at Coventry University (expected completion: June 2026) specialising in end-to-end (E2E) network slicing for 5G/6G. My work sits at the intersection of mobile network architecture, resource management, and AI-enabled orchestration, informed by prior industry roles in telecom operations and core-network engineering. I am preparing for industry R\&D / advanced engineering roles in networking and telecom, with interests spanning network automation/orchestration, NFV/SDN, resource allocation, MEC/task offloading, and deep reinforcement learning for closed-loop network control.

My research focuses on developing efficient mobile network architectures and resource management strategies, drawing on my industrial experience in telecommunication networks, especially in core networks. I aim to contribute to the advancement of 5G/6G technologies through academic research, industrial applications, and standardization efforts. My research interests include 5G/6G, network slicing, NFV, SDN, resource allocation, AI-enabled network management and orchestration, MEC, task offloading, deep reinforcement learning, and mobile network design.
