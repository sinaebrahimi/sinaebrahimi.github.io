About Me

What I am all about.

I am a PhD candidate at Coventry University, UK, specialising in End-to-End (E2E) Network Slicing for next-generation 6G networks. My doctoral research centres on the ENSURE framework — a multi-agent deep reinforcement learning system that jointly manages resources across the Radio Access Network (RAN), Transport Network (TN), and Core Network (CN) domains operating at different timescales.

Prior to my PhD, I accumulated extensive industry experience in telecommunications, spanning network engineering, RF planning and optimisation, SDN/NFV integration, protocol validation, and V2X systems integration. I am passionate about bridging the gap between academic research in AI/ML and practical deployment in future communication networks.
